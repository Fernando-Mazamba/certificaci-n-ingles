Frequently Asked Questions

💰 Do you really pay after the certificate?
Yes, Continental Consulting only invoices after the international certificate is issued and delivered to your company. You pay only after having the certificate in hand, with no financial risk.
💻 Is the audit completely remote?
Yes, 100% online through a digital platform. No travel required, saving time and costs. You only need a computer with a camera and internet connection.
🌍 Will my certificate be valid for international tenders?
Absolutely. We have globally recognized UAF, IAF, and IAS accreditations. Your certificate will be accepted in international tenders and contracts.
📅 How long does the complete process take?
The standard process takes 15 to 30 days. For urgent cases, we can issue the certificate in less than 15 business days, subject to spot availability.
💳 What payment methods do you accept after the certificate?
We accept bank transfer, credit/debit cards, PayPal, and up to 3 interest-free installments. All after you receive your certificate.
📄 What documentation do I need to start?
You only need to complete our request form. Our team will guide you through every step and provide all necessary documentation for the audit.
🔄 Does the certification require annual renewal?
Yes, ISO certification is valid for 3 years, with annual surveillance audits. We offer maintenance plans with preferential conditions for our clients.
⏱️ What does "response in less than 2 hours" mean?
It means that after submitting your request, a specialized consultant will contact you via WhatsApp or email within a maximum of 2 hours on business days, to present your personalized proposal.
🏢 Which countries do you operate in?
We serve English-speaking countries globally, including the Caribbean, Africa, Asia & Middle East, Oceania, and Europe. Contact us for any country not listed.
